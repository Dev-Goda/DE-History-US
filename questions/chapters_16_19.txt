CHAPTER: Synthesis

Q: Which of the following BEST represents a similarity between the Knights of Labor and the Populist Party?

2: Both sought broad economic reform and challenged concentrated wealth
3: Both supported the gold standard

ANSWER: 2

Q: Social Darwinism and the "Lost Cause" ideology both served to:
1: Justify existing social hierarchies and resist reform
2: Promote equality and democratic values
3: Strengthen labor unions and farmer movements
4: Encourage government intervention in the economy
ANSWER: 1

Q: The Dawes Act and the Chinese Exclusion Act both reflect which broader late 19th-century trend?
1: American generosity toward minority groups
2: Federal government policies that restricted or undermined non-white groups
3: Growing immigration from East Asia
4: Expansion of civil rights following the Civil War
ANSWER: 2
